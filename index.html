What I do

By day, I am a partner and designer in These Ways. A design studio, where we specialize in digital product development. I have worked with clients ranging from CNBC, Reuters, TV2, Equinor, Beerenberg, iFlix, Ditio, Sensario, Aquabyte and centers for psychologial health in Norway.

Latest finished project is a Virtual Reality pilot for on site training.

In Vimond I was lead designer for Vimond IO, a browser based 4K video editor. For CNBC our team designed and implemented an archival and retrieval system for live broadcast, indexing closed captioning for efficient search. In TV 2 Sumo I did a redesign of their app for Android, with offline support and other new features.
